Raindrops

July 22, 2026 1 min read Poem
◌ ◌ ◌

Skies shine bright, each day a brand-new start.

Some days grow dark, and test the strongest heart.

Perfect days are never guaranteed,

Yet every trial plants another seed.

This world is filled with endless chaos.

Many wander, burdened by their loss.

Yet hope is real, though unseen like a ghost.

Hold on to it, for it will cost the least.

We live within a cycle full of wonder,

Flowing gently, much like running water.

Take the falling rain as an example.

It cleanses the earth and helps new life grow.

Starting again is nature's quiet way.

Sometimes it will leave behind deep pain.

Yet accepting every scar and stain

Is the bravest choice you'll ever make.
