About the Author

Ayhen A. Narciso is a journalism student at Bulacan State University and a student journalist with a deep appreciation for stories that connect people. While journalism has taught him to seek truth and tell stories with purpose, creative writing has become a personal passion where thoughts, emotions, and imagination find a home. Through essays, poems, and fiction, he continues to explore the beauty of everyday experiences, quiet reflections, and the human condition, believing that every story has the power to leave a lasting impression.

“I write to preserve what memory almost loses.”

✦ About the Project

The Unsent Letters & Stories

The Unsent Letters & Stories is an independent literary project created and maintained by Ayhen A. Narciso. It was built as a quiet home for original essays, poems, stories, and reflections, preserving moments that deserve to be remembered and shared. Every piece invites readers to slow down, reflect, and discover comfort in words that speak to ordinary yet meaningful experiences.

As this collection continues to grow, so does its purpose. More than a personal archive, it hopes to become a warm and welcoming literary space where readers and writers connect through meaningful stories. The website embraces opportunities, future collaborations, and creative partnerships through Letters Between Us, fostering a safe, respectful, and inclusive community where creativity is celebrated, ideas are shared, and every voice is encouraged to learn, grow, inspire, and be heard.
