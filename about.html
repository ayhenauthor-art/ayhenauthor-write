About the Author

Welcome to my little corner of the internet.

My name is Ayhen Narciso, and I write to preserve moments that words often fail to explain. My work explores love, memory, identity, healing, hope, and the quiet beauty found in ordinary lives.

The Unsent Letters & Stories is more than a collection of writings—it is a home for thoughts that were never spoken aloud and stories that deserve to be remembered.

Whether you've come here by accident or intention, thank you for spending a little of your time with my words.

“I write to preserve what memory almost loses.”
